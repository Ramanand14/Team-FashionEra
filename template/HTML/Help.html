I have forgotten my password.
When signing in, click on the "Forgot Password" button. You will be redirected to a new page where you need to fill in your registered email address and new password and soon after you will receive an email with a password given in your mail.
I want to delete my account
To delete your account click on delete account button and enter your email id and password and click on delete account button and after that you will get the message on top of your website like your account is deleted successsfully.
I am unable to login to my account.
You can recover your password using the "Forgot Password" button given on login page. If you are still unable to access your account, then please contact to our customer care.
Does FashionEra read my emails?
No. FashioEra does not read your emails.
